Glossary

Dive into our glossary to better understand key concepts surrounding time, clocks, and quantum theories. This guide offers clear and accessible definitions to explore more deeply the notions discussed in our articles. Whether you are a novice or enthusiast, this glossary will help enrich your experience with Echoes of Time.

Time

A fundamental concept that denotes the continuous progression of events, from the past to the future. Time is often perceived as linear, but contemporary theories, such as those arising from quantum physics, challenge this traditional view.

Clock

An instrument used to measure, display, or track the passage of time. From the first mechanical clocks to modern atomic devices, clocks have evolved to offer increasingly greater precision.

Quantum

Derived from quantum physics, a branch of physics that studies phenomena at the subatomic particle scale. In this context, quantum time refers to microscopic fluctuations and notions of temporal indeterminacy.

Time Travel

A science fiction concept that envisions the possibility of moving through time, whether to the past or the future. Although time travel is a popular theme in literature and cinema, it is based on complex and speculative scientific theories.

Evolution

The process of gradual transformation and change over time. In the context of clocks and time, evolution can refer to the improvement of time-measuring devices or the changing theories about the nature of time itself.
