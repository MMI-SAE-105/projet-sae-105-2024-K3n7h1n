My Selection

Discover my captivating selection of films and series exploring the mystery of time in all its forms. Whether through stories of time travel, fascinating paradoxes, or reflections on the flow of time, each work invites you to think and immerse yourself in this complex universe. Prepare for an unforgettable cinematic experience where past, present, and future intertwine.

In Edge of Tomorrow, an inexperienced soldier, William Cage, finds himself caught in a time loop after being killed fighting invading alien forces. With each death, he relives the same day, gaining new skills and moving closer to a solution to defeat the enemy. With the help of warrior Rita Vrataski, he must break this cycle and save humanity before time runs out.

In Loki, after escaping the Avengers, the god of mischief is captured by the Time Variance Authority (TVA), an organization that monitors the timeline. Forced to collaborate, Loki explores the mysteries of time, the multiverse, and questions his own identity while facing threats that could disrupt the balance of the universe.

"Interstellar" (2014) approaches time from the scientific perspective of relativity. The film follows a group of astronauts traveling through space to find a new habitable planet. A striking scene illustrates time dilation: on a planet near a black hole, one hour equals seven years on Earth. This cinematic representation of Einstein’s theory highlights the effects of gravity on time.

In Groundhog Day, Phil Connors, a cynical weatherman, finds himself stuck reliving the same day during a report in a small town. As he goes through various phases of frustration, despair, and attempts at personal improvement, he must learn to change his perspective and make the most of each day to escape the time loop.

In Stranger Things, a group of children in a small American town discovers a series of supernatural events after their friend Will disappears. As they meet a girl with psychic powers named Eleven, they realize their town is linked to a terrifying parallel dimension called the Upside Down. Together, they face monstrous creatures and secret forces to save their friend and protect their world from unknown dangers.
