About Echos of Time

Echos of Time is a site dedicated to exploring humanity's internal clock, tracing various temporal elements such as music, literature, and cinema.

Definition of the Clock

"The clock, a timeless symbol of time measurement, rhythms our lives by capturing every passing moment. From the simple tick-tock to the frenetic race of the hands, it reminds us that time never stops but continues to flow, pushing us to seize the present moment."
