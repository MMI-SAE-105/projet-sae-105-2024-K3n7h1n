About

Welcome to Echoes of Time, your premier destination to explore the captivating universe of clocks and time. Through detailed articles, focuses on unexpected aspects, and a modern look at our temporal perceptions, we offer a space for exchange and reflection.

Our Vision

At Echoes of Time, we offer a series of articles that trace the evolution of clocks, whether the first mechanical clocks or quantum technologies related to time. Through our publications, you will also discover the influence of music, cinema (notably Christopher Nolan or the movie Your Name) and literature on our temporal perceptions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make the complex concept of time more accessible and fascinating for everyone. Whether you are passionate about horology, curious about quantum sciences, or simply intrigued by how time influences our daily lives, Echoes of Time offers you a variety of resources to deepen your knowledge and satisfy your curiosity.

Our Goals

  • Present the history and evolution of clocks in an educational manner.
  • Create connections between different disciplines (music, cinema, science...).
  • Provide a space for exchange and discussion around time.
  • Stimulate curiosity and open-mindedness.

Our Chronological Journey

Discover how we have evolved since the beginning:

  • 2023: Initial idea Echoes of Time and first research on horology.
  • 2024: Launch of version 1 of the site, with musical and literary articles.
  • 2025: Addition of the cinema section, focus on Christopher Nolan and Your Name.
  • 2026: Collaboration with experts in quantum physics and expansion of our content.
