Evolution of the Clock

Explore our chronological timeline and dive into the fascinating history of clocks and the evolution of time. From Antiquity to modern quantum theories, this timeline guides you through the key events that have shaped our understanding of time, in a playful and illustrated manner of the major stages of this millennial evolution.
