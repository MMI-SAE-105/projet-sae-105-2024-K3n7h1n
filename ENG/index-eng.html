About Echos of Time

Echos of Time is a website dedicated to exploring humanity’s internal clock, tracing different temporal elements such as music, literature, and cinema.

Definition of Paraplage

“Paraplage” refers to an abstract concept of time, inviting us to reflect on the relativity of events and our perception of the past, present, and future.
